ABOUT

Best Before is an app that helps you reduce your food waste. It tracks the best before and use by dates on your brought food and reminds you to use it before it goes off. Best Before uses bracode scanning to collect information about the food you buy. Making you aware of how much food waste your are creating.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Best Before is ideal for those who want to reduce their food waste. It’s great for people who buy in bulk and forget what have in the back of the pantry. Not only does it help you reduce your food waste but also helps you save money.

Key Features

Best Before allows you to keep track of the best before and use by dates on the food you buy. You scan the barcode of your item and it then set the best before date into the calander where it reminds what is about to go past its use by or best before date.

You can search for a past food item you have brought and just update the best before or use by date. Best Before categories your food so it is easy to find what you need.

With food that has gone past its best before date it gets sorted into the “Past Date” where it shows you all the food that has gone past its date and how much longer you have until you need to throw it away.
