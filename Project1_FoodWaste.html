TARGET AUDIENCE

With Best Before you scan the best before dates of food and it reminds you multiple times before its goes off. With Best Before you never have to remember when the food goes off ever again. By downloading Best Before you are actively helping to reduce food waste, and save money as well.

How it works

Best Before scans the best before date of your food sets up reminders in its database to tell you when something is still before its date or has passed its date. Depending on how long it has before the date, the app will set a different amount of reminders. For example if a products best before date is in a week. The app will set up 3 reminders before the date and on the day it will let you know that the best before day is today. After it the best before date the product will get put into the After section of the app. The After section allows you to see what has passed its best before date and lets you decide to keep it or dispose of it.

Colour Scheme

Best Before uses fresh and vibrant tones to go along with the idea of combatting food waste. The vibrant tones will highlight the different sections. White and grey will keep it simple and easy to follow.

Font

A clear clean font for clean green New Zealand. Open Sans light for the main text to keep it easy to read, while Roboto for the headings for something sharp and eye catching.
